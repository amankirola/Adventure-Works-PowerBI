# AdventureWorks Sales Report – Power BI
A sales analytics dashboard built using the AdventureWorks sample dataset in Power BI Desktop. The report covers business performance across 2020–2022, broken into four pages.


---

## Pages

1. Main Dashboard 
Overview of company-wide KPIs at a glance.

  - Total Revenue: $24.9M | Profit: $10.5M | Orders: 25.2K | Return Rate: 2.17%
  - Revenue trend line from Jan 2020 to Jan 2022
  - Monthly snapshots for revenue, orders, and returns vs previous month
  - Top 10 products table with order count, revenue, and return percentage

2. Map View
Geographic breakdown of sales performance.

  - Covers 6 countries: United States, Canada, United Kingdom, France, Germany, Australia
  - Filterable by region — Europe, North America, and Pacific

3. Product Analysis
Deep dive into individual product performance.

  - Tracks orders, revenue, and profit against monthly targets
  - Profit vs adjusted profit chart to simulate the effect of price changes
  - Useful for spotting underperforming products early

4. Customer Analysis
Customer segmentation and revenue analysis.

  - 17.4K unique customers | $1,431 avg revenue per customer
  - Orders broken down by income level and occupation
  - Top 100 customers ranked by revenue
  - Top customer: Mr. Maurice Shan — 6 orders, $12,408

    
--- 

## Notable Insights

- **Accessories** had the highest order volume *(17K)*, followed by **Bikes** *(13.9K)* and **Clothing** *(7K)*
- **Tires** and **Tubes** was the most ordered product type.
- **Shorts** had the highest return rate
- Revenue showed consistent growth from early 2020 through early 2022, with a noticeable spike around mid-2021
- Average income customers placed the most orders *(11.6K)*, with **Professionals** leading by occupation *(7.9K)*
