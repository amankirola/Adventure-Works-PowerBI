## Notable Insights

- **Accessories** had the highest order volume *(17K)*, followed by **Bikes** *(13.9K)* and **Clothing** *(7K)*
- **Tires** and **Tubes** was the most ordered product type.
- **Shorts** had the highest return rate
- Revenue showed consistent growth from early 2020 through early 2022, with a noticeable spike around mid-2021
- Average income customers placed the most orders *(11.6K)*, with **Professionals** leading by occupation *(7.9K)*
